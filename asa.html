About League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA, which stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena.
It is a game where two teams fight each other to destroy the other team's base, or 'nexus', using a variety of characters, each with different skills and roles.
It was first released in October 27, 2009, and by 2012 it was the most popular game in Europe and North America in terms of hours played.
By September 2016 the company who made the game, Riot Games, estimated over 100 million active players in League of Legends.
League of Legends also has one of the biggest eSports scenes, starting in 2011 with the first professional tournament, the Season 1 Championship in Dreamhack at Sweden.
It has now grown to many tournaments, held by Riot Games.
In 2016, the professional League of Legends gaming scene had a prize pool of nearly $30 million, and the 2017 World Championships had a reported 60 million unique viewers.
